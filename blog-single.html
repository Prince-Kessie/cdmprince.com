CDM develops you to be a good Leader and Manager.

Leadership is about influence; it’s about having a character within that can be an undergirding support structure as you build other lives around you. Leadership is not about being perfect, it is about the ability to learn, to be accountable, and to apply and exemplify what you’ve learned and then pass it along to the lives around you. Leadership is your ability to let wisdom live through you.

Leadership is knowing how to lead the life you’ve been given by God. We’ve referred to it as self-leadership many times. It is a life surrendered to the Lordship of Jesus Christ; a lifelong event, not a one-time action. The “self” in self-leadership refers to taking ownership and responsibility for your own life, not waiting for someone else to fix or change you or your situations.

"There are multiple ways a person can lead. This is because there are multiple personality, character, experience, and gifting blends that can make up a leader. The goal is to be the leader God created YOU to be, letting Christ reveal Himself uniquely through you to the Body and the world."
