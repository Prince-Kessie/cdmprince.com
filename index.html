Our Unique Features courses

The best is here for you; become a member today.

Sure Foundation

The Word of God is meant to be the Foundation and standard of our lives. This course will teach you the important, value, and integrity of God's Word.

The Basic of Righteousness

Righteousness is not something that you do to become, It is something that you believe to become. This course will teach you how to become righteous in God's sight.

The Components of Salvation

Salvation is everything that Jesus died to provide for humanity. This course will teach you the eigth components of salvation.

The Love of God

God so love humanity that He gave His only Son to be the atoning sacrifice our sins. This course will teach you God's love for you, not your love for Him or for others either.

The Principles of Financial Prosperity

Prosperity is one of the components of salvation. Jesus took away our poverty so that we can receive His prosperity. This course will teach you how you can prosper financially.

The Principles of Grace and Faith

Grace and faith work together to produce salvation. They are never independent of each other! This course will teach you the balance between grace and faith.
